# Privacy Policy  
Last updated: January 2025

LiftLog (“the App”) is a personal fitness tracking application designed to store workout and bodyweight data directly on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is stored, and how it is used.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data  
The App **does not collect**, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII).  
No account creation is required.

### 1.2 Fitness Data  
The App stores the following data **locally on your device**:

- Personal Records (exercise name, weight, reps, date)
- Bodyweight entries (weight, date)
- Unit preference (lbs or kg)

This data never leaves your device unless you manually export it or share it.

### 1.3 Device or Usage Data  
The App does **not** collect analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers.  
No data is sent to external servers.

---

## 2. How Your Data Is Stored

All saved information is stored in the App’s **internal storage**, which is:

- Private to the App  
- Not accessible by other apps  
- Deleted permanently when you uninstall LiftLog  

The App does not use cloud services, external servers, or third-party storage.

---

## 3. How Your Data Is Used

The data you enter is used **solely for displaying progress inside the App**.  
LiftLog does not process, share, or sell any data to third parties.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use:

- Third-party SDKs  
- Advertising services  
- Analytics providers  
- External APIs  

No third-party has access to your data.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

LiftLog does not collect personal information from users of any age, including children.  
The App is suitable for general audiences.

---

## 6. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally, privacy risks are minimal.  
However, users are responsible for securing their device (e.g., screen lock, backups).

---

## 7. Your Rights

Since no data is collected or stored externally:

- We cannot access your data  
- We cannot modify or delete data for you  
- All data management must be done directly within the app or by uninstalling the app

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements.  
Any new version will be included in the App listing and documentation.
